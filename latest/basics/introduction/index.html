CryptoXpress Documentation

Overview​

CryptoXpress simplifies your Crypto and Blockchain experience, providing instant access to core features like Trading, NFTs, and Wallets through its application. So you can spend your time wisely, focusing on what matters most, with the convenience of CryptoXpress.

CryptoXpress Wallet serves as a digital wallet where users can securely store, send and recieve Binance Coins(BNB) and various Binance-based tokens (BEP-20). You no longer need to maintain several wallet applications.

The CryptoXpress application offers a wide array of features, including cryptocurrency trading, staking, flight and hotel booking, NFT purchases, and effortless fund transfers to family and friends, all with just a few taps.
